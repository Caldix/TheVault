Sending from WhatsApp

  1. On your phone, open this site's link, then your browser menu → Add to Home screen (this step is required — it only works once installed this way).
  2. In WhatsApp, open the photo or message from your husband.
  3. Tap Share (the arrow icon) → choose Memories from the list of apps.
  4. It'll open this site with the photo/text already attached to a new post — just hit Post.

This only works on Android with Chrome. If "Memories" doesn't show up in the share list, make sure you completed step 1, then try again.
